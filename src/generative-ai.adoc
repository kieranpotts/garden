= Generative AI

Generative AI refers to *link:./machine-learning.adoc[machine learning]* systems that produce new content — text, images, video, audio, 3D models, code, and more — rather than simply classifying or analysing existing data. These systems are trained on large datasets and learn to generate plausible new outputs by modelling the statistical patterns in that data.

*link:./large-language-model.adoc[Large language models (LLMs)]* are the most prominent example, generating text and code. But generative AI encompasses other modalities too. This sections covers the broader landscape of generative AI, including image, video, and audio generation.

toc::[]

== Image generation

Image generation models produce photorealistic or stylised images from text prompts, reference images, or other inputs. Most modern image generators are based on diffusion models, which learn to iteratively denoise random noise into coherent images.

Notable tools and platforms include:

* https://www.midjourney.com/[Midjourney] — High-quality image generation accessible via Discord and web interface.

* https://openai.com/dall-e-3[DALL·E] — OpenAI's image generation model, integrated into ChatGPT.

* https://stability.ai/[Stable Diffusion] — Open-weight diffusion model from Stability AI, widely used as a base for fine-tuned variants.

* https://www.comfy.org/[ComfyUI] — Open-source, node-based visual workflow engine for running diffusion models locally, supporting image, video, 3D, and audio generation pipelines.

== Video generation

Video generation models extend image generation into the temporal dimension, producing short clips from text or image prompts.

Notable tools and platforms include:

* https://openai.com/sora[Sora] — OpenAI's text-to-video model.

* https://www.runwayml.com/[Runway] — Commercial video generation and editing platform.

* https://lumalabs.ai/[Luma Dream Machine] — Text-to-video and image-to-video generation.

== Audio generation

Audio generation covers music, sound effects, and voice synthesis from text or other audio inputs.

Notable tools and platforms include:

* https://suno.ai/[Suno] — AI music generation from text prompts.

* https://www.udio.com/[Udio] — Music generation platform.

* https://elevenlabs.io/[ElevenLabs] — Voice synthesis and cloning, widely used for narration and dubbing.
